### About Me
I'm a Ph.D candidate at Australian National University,where I work in the [Sound, Music and Creative Computing (SMCC) Lab](https://smcclab.au/) supervised by Dr. [Charles Martin](https://charlesmartin.au/), Dr. [Christian Walder](https://christianwalder.com/), and Dr. [Jing Zhang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Qa1DMv8AAAAJ&hl=en). I hold a Master’s degree in Machine Learning and Computer Vision from the Australian National University and a Bachelor’s degree in Statistics from Hong Kong Baptist University.
